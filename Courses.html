RGIFIRE Kitchenhood Fire Suppression System

Rahman Group Fire Division supply and installation of kitchen hood fire suppression systems in Qatar. In modern commercial kitchens, the risk of fire is very high. So a wet chemical fire suppression system is necessary specially made for the kitchen.


Rahman Group Modern commercial kitchens are protected by wet chemical suppression systems against possible hazards including open flames, extremely hot cooking surfaces, and environments that are extensively greased, which can result in oil and grease fires. Different cylinder sizes There are models NF-KS5, NF-KS10, NF-KS15, and NF-KS20 with varying flow capacities. applications: safeguarding Kitchen hoods Plenums Filters Ducts cooking utensils such woks, upright broilers, gas radiant char broilers, upright broilers, and deep-fat fryers
